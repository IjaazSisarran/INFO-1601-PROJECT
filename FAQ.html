FAQs

How does the website work?

The website allows customers to view and purchase products from local sellers. Customers can browse products, contact sellers to arrange a meeting, and leave reviews for products and sellers. Sellers can create an account, list their products, and include their contact information on their page, which may include a phone number or email address. Customers can use this contact information to communicate with the seller.

Can I contact sellers directly through the website?

No, you can contact sellers using their contact information provided on their page.

How do I arrange a meeting with a seller?

You can arrange a meeting with a seller by calling or emailing them and agreeing on a time and location to meet.

Can I see the seller's location on a map?

No, the website features a map that customers and sellers can you use to agree on a meet-up location.

How do I add items to my shopping cart on your website?

To add items to your shopping cart on our website, simply click on the "Add to Cart" button that is usually located next to or underneath the product picture. This will add the item to your cart and you can continue shopping or proceed to checkout when you're ready. You can also adjust the quantity of the item in your cart or remove it entirely before completing your purchase.

Can I leave a review of a product or seller?

Yes, customers can leave reviews of products and sellers on the website.

How can I create a seller account and set up my own shop?

To create a seller account and set up your own shop, you will need to register on the website and upload your products to the Shop page

What are the fees for using the website as a seller?

The website is free for both buyers and sellers to use.

Is there a limit to how many products I can list as a seller?

There is no limit to how many products you can list as a seller on the website, but we recommend that you do not spam or repost the same item for sale until at least a month has passed. This helps to ensure a fair and respectful marketplace for all users.

How do I get paid for my sales as a seller?

The website does not handle any financial transactions between buyers and sellers. Payment for products is made in person at the agreed meet-up location between the buyer and seller. The website does not store or process any payment information, and is not responsible for any issues or disputes that may arise between buyers and sellers during the transaction.

What are the safety measures in place for meeting up with a seller?

The website recommends meeting in public places and taking precautions such as bringing a friend or family member with you.

Can I report suspicious activity on the website?

Yes, you can report suspicious activity on the website by contacting customer support.

What if I have a problem with a transaction?

If you have a problem with a transaction, we recommend that you try to resolve the issue directly with the seller. Since transactions are made in person at the agreed meet-up location, the website may not be able to assist in resolving issues. However, if you are unable to resolve the issue with the seller, you can contact customer support for assistance and we will do our best to help you..

How do I delete my account or shop if I no longer want to use the website?

You can delete your account or shop by contacting customer support.

We connect buyers and sellers in their community. Our website is a safe and easy platform for buying and selling products locally, free for everyone to use. Support local businesses with us.
