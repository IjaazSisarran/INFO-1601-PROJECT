About Us

Zucky Wuckies

Zucky Wuckies is an innovative company behind the online marketplace JIIVE, created by four individuals with a shared vision for transforming the way people shop and sell online. Josiah, Elijah, Ijaaz, and Varune are the masterminds behind JIIVE, each bringing their unique skills and expertise to the table. Together, they have created a platform that offers a seamless shopping experience for buyers and sellers alike. While users can browse and discover items on the JIIVE website, Zucky Wuckies has a unique approach to transactions - all purchases are completed in-person, allowing for a more personalized and secure exchange. This approach sets JIIVE apart from other online marketplaces, as it provides an added level of safety and comfort for both buyers and sellers. With their dedication to innovation and customer satisfaction, Zucky Wuckies has become the go-to company for those who want a safe and hassle-free online marketplace experience through their platform JIIVE.

We connect buyers and sellers in their community. Our website is a safe and easy platform for buying and selling products locally, free for everyone to use. Support local businesses with us.
